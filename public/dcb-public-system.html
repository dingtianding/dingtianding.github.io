DCB Public: system design

The consumer front door: a vetted money & investing knowledge base with a citation-grounded AI advisor. It answers only from a vetted corpus and shows its sources, so people get the right answer instead of a confidently wrong one. Educational, not advice. (This is the engine behind the /ask demo.)

Legend Public service Database 👤 User External / sibling request entitlement · feed
The flow: the general public (Stripe subscribers) and comped firm clients hit a Next.js front-end that calls a FastAPI backend. Browsing serves vetted, cited explainers from a Postgres corpus; asking runs a RAG advisor that retrieves from that corpus and generates a cited answer with Claude, checkable, not confidently wrong, and clearly educational rather than personalized advice. Stripe gates subscriptions; DCB Practice supplies entitlement for comped clients, outbound only, no shared database; and DCB Research distills cited entries into the corpus. This backend is exactly what the /ask demo previews.
