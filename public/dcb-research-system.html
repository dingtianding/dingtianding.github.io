DCB Research: system design

The grounded source of truth for the suite. It ingests SEC filings + tax sources, normalizes them into Postgres, and answers questions with hybrid retrieval and cited generation, with an eval harness that measures both, so quality is proven, not assumed.

Legend Research service Database Source / external Consumer product data flow measures · distills
The pipeline: SEC submissions and XBRL company facts (plus federal/state tax sources) are ingested to object storage and normalized into a Postgres corpus of filings, facts, and sections. That corpus drives two things: an analytics dashboard (revenue, margin, debt, liquidity, risk-text changes, filing diffs) and a hybrid-retrieval layer, semantic embeddings (a dependency-free hashing embedder, no API key) plus BM25 keyword, fused with reciprocal-rank fusion, that feeds a grounded answer citing the exact sections it used. An eval harness scores retrieval (hit@k, offline) and generation (groundedness via an LLM judge) and runs in CI, so regressions are caught, not shipped. Distilled cited entries flow to DCB Public, and procedural how-to feeds DCB Practice's firm knowledge base.
